Process Approach

What is this project about?

Wikipedia is one of the oldest and most used virtual encyclopedias, over the years there haven't been many changes in its interface, although it is still the most visited when it comes to consulting some specific data, the problem is that ti is only for personal use and ti isn't well seen to use this information for school projects. Finally users are not Log in either.

What we want to find?

This research aims to find out why the user feels that this website is not so credible when it comes to using it for school projects and how to go about getting more logins to the website.

Polling methods

Online self-administered structured poll. Done anonymously and confidentially. Sampling procedure: Non-probabilistic. In this research, we focus on u s e r s between the ages of 16 and 18 who are in high school or have recently completed it.
