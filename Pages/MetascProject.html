Metasc

Ordering your homework is easier

Metasc is a mobile application that allows you to organize daily tasks and activities very easily and, in turn, prioritize the most urgent ones. According to the desk research for this project, the applications that exist in the market to generate an orderly workflow are not intended for young people starting high school, so this app captured a young sector whose need was a low-complexity app. , a key point that differentiates it from others.

Process Approach

Context

With the pandemic we could see how essential organization is when it comes to make virtual meetings, presenting papers, organizing with other students, even when doing chores. It seems that we cannot ignore that young people have more technological knowledge than adults, in fact they are digital natives We also know how the organization impacts this new post-pandemic world, where we need to be connected without having to be in the same place. For this, we thought of making our contribution to the student's toolbox with an essential application to keep tasks in order and acquire habits along the way.

Problems

The apps for organizing homework, which are available so far, are too complex for children starting high school. They are designed for a professional environment and are based on the kanban method, which makes the application require several columns in parallel.

Solution

The proposal offers a tool that captivates by its colors, to a colorful audience and is based on the system of lists, with a key function, automatically prioritize the next task to perform. Order is the main strategy, a quality that improves the quality of a society.
