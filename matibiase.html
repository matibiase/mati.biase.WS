Mati Biase UX/UI Designer

Hi how are you ?

It is a pleasure to see you here, I would like to invite you to get to know me better. My name is Matias but I prefer to be called Mati, I studied Industrial Design, while studying I discovered a particular interest in the sensitive part of the world of design, especially in the connections between people and the experience they live thanks to design, I think in experience as the door to success in every way.

When I was in college I started thinking about the importance of living the whole design experience, so with a partner we made a design company, halfis. It was a great experience where we learned a lot while we continued studying and I worked there almost 4 years. The best part was that we were able to apply the theory in real life and get the skills to communicate with people in the project design process, from the customer to the worker who made the product.

Along the way I was discovering my qualities and interests. I observed that there are many problems when it is time to adopt teaching and school subjects to the new great change that technologies bring and especially to the lack of education on the management of our mental skills. It was at that time that I began to study neuroscience and educational methodologies, on my own. Finally, at this point in my life I studied Ux/Ui, motivated to find a sustainable way to produce solutions and generating that people feel motivated to continue using them with the help of experience as a main strategy.

Story Line

UX/UI Design

Directing my career in relation to the virtual product was my way of converging my passion with the generation of products that generate less waste and to create products with "life", since the virtual product is always under constant review and change.

Html5

The decision to aim to learn code is given by the importance and the search to be more efficient when designing, thus being a way to find key points that streamline design. On the other hand no less is a subject that I wanted to learn if or if.

UX/UI Design avanzado

In this second stage delve into research tools and concepts of designers such as Don Norman or the Ux Laws, which allow to make a product more focused on the user and how it will interact with our product.

UX/UI Design

At this stage learn and delve into design tools dedicated to experience and interface design.

halfis

Halfis was my personal project and my source of experimentation to learn to relate with real customers and suppliers, as well as to develop my tools in real environments.

Havita

In halfis we launched a service called Havita where were the last stages more advanced, this was oriented to the design of the experience in the home, modifying spaces so that people feel more at home. In hindsight it was the intermediate point between the physical product and the move to service virtuality.

Figuretti

Figuretti is the first physical product we made, it consisted of a folded sheet metal oven which cooked on the basis of wood. This was patented by our study and marketed nationally.

Industrial Design

The fact of having studied a career as a design frame my bases as a person, undoubtedly a before and after, at this point I became more critical in every aspect of life, design is part of every decision I take.
